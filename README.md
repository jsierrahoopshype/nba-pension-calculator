## Pension Scale (2024 CBA)

| Years of Service | Annual Pension at Age 62 |
|------------------|-------------------------|
| 3 years | $56,988 |
| 4 years | $79,561 |
| 5 years | $102,134 |
| 6 years | $124,707 |
| 7 years | $147,281 |
| 8 years | $169,854 |
| 9 years | $192,427 |
| 10+ years | $215,000 (maximum) |

Players can start collecting at **age 45** (with reduced benefits) or wait until **age 62** for the full amount.

## Other NBA Benefits

- **401(k)**: 140% match (up to $32,200/year)
- **Tuition Reimbursement**: $62,500/year for 3+ year players
- **Health Benefits**: Medical, dental, vision, life insurance
